Understanding the Birir Residence's Energy Needs

The first step to energy independence is understanding current consumption. The Birir residence has a significant daily energy demand, primarily driven by lighting and essential appliances. This detailed audit forms the basis for a perfectly sized solar solution.

Daily Energy Consumption Breakdown (22.87 kWh)

Peak Power Demand Analysis

While daily energy usage is high, the potential peak power demand from all appliances running at once is nearly 19kW. The proposed 8kW inverter is sized to handle typical, not maximum, simultaneous loads, using smart prioritization.

Total Potential Peak Load: 17,998 W
Proposed Inverter Capacity: 8,000 W
Key High-Wattage Items: Dishwasher (2000W), Cooker (2000W), Vacuum (1200W)

The Proposed Solar Solution

To meet and exceed the residence's energy needs, a powerful and reliable hybrid solar system is proposed. It combines high-output solar panels, a smart hybrid inverter, and significant battery storage to ensure power day and night, even during grid outages.
